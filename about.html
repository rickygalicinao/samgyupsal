About Us

Our Story

The Korean barbecue craze has been in Manila for a while, and it looks like shops specializing in the stuff are more than a passing trend — they’re here to stay.

Leann’s Teahouse is a newly opened Korean restaurant run by a big family of food-loving entrepreneurs.

Located in northern Quezon City, it is a Tipsy samgyeopsal restaurant, which offers unlimited Korean barbecue with prices ranging from P350 to P900, depending on the type of meat and marinade you order.

Definitely worth a visit!
